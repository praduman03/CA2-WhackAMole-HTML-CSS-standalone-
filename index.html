How to Play?

Instructions

  • There are six holes in the play area.
  • A mole randomly pops out from any of these six holes.
  • Points are scored by whacking the mole as it appears.
  • The faster the reation the higher the score.
  • The final score is based on the number of moles the player has struck.
  • There will be a 15 sec time limit.
  • ALL THE BEST!!!
